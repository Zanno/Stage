Internal pages

The design of internal screens fits perfectly with both of the home page proposals.

Products

The items are shown on a grid disposition giving importance to the middle work space. But at the same time, left navigation is also clearly presented to browse and filter the items of that work space. All the necessary information is shown to the user, keeping the user options as much simple and intuitive as possible.

Collateral

List view takes up too much space because we need to show a lot of information at the begining. That list should be filtered by the user, so he or she uses the left side menu to apply those filters, having highlighted the selected options with the green branding color. The purpose of the design is to keep a clear interface while pointing out selected or filtered items and calls to action.
