This is a more minimalistic and clean approach, focusing not on US (visual branding) but on THEM (user interaction). Rounded shapes for the tiles with the icons inside, movement of the lines and visual changes in tiles when hovering with the mouse pointer help to give that sense of dynamism and interaction, promoting the user to click on them. The interactions when choosing each option create in the user an energy boost, supported by the relaxation and tranquility of the clean and flat background avoiding figures or colours that could distract them.
